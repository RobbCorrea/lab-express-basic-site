Have you ever been to a Robocup match?


Robocup is an inter-college robotics contest where students from different universities build their robots and make them battle in different competitions.

There's Robo-Soccer, where basically students build Lego robots programed to chase a ball emitting infrared light.

Then there's Mini-sumo

This is somewhat the favorite for many mechatronics and robotics students. Why? Because building robots can be fairly costly. Even building small robots is not cheap. So, building a Mini-sumo robot, is both, accesible and a great challenges for newcomer engineering students.
